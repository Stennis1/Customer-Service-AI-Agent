Common Account Issues:

1. Forgot Password:
   - Click "Forgot Password" on login page.
   - Enter registered email.
   - Follow reset link sent to your inbox.

2. Email Not Verified:
   - Check spam folder.
   - Resend verification email from login screen.

3. Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):
   - Enabled under Account Settings.
   - Requires authentication app (e.g., Google Authenticator).

4. Account Locked:
   - After 5 failed login attempts.
   - Automatically unlocks after 30 minutes.
   - Contact support if urgent access is needed.
