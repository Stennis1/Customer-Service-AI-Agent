To get started with AdillisEdu:

1. Create an account at https://adillisedu.com
2. Verify your email address.
3. Complete your school profile:
   - School name
   - Address
   - Academic calendar
4. Add teachers and administrative staff.
5. Import students via CSV upload or manual entry.
6. Configure class structure and subjects.
7. Enable parent portal access if needed.

Recommended First Actions:
- Set up grading structure.
- Configure fee categories.
- Assign teachers to classes.
