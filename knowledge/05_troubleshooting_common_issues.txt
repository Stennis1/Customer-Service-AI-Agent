Common Issues and Fixes:

1. Attendance Not Saving:
   - Check internet connection.
   - Refresh browser and retry.
   - Ensure teacher has attendance permission.

2. Report Card Not Generating:
   - Ensure grades are published.
   - Confirm grading structure is configured.

3. Parent Cannot Login:
   - Confirm invitation email was accepted.
   - Reset parent password.

4. CSV Upload Fails:
   - Ensure format matches provided template.
   - Check for missing required columns.
